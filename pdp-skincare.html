A feather-light, invisible shield that protects against UVA/UVB rays and blue light while targeting age spots and uneven skin tone. The ultra-fluid formula absorbs instantly with zero white cast, making it the perfect daily base.

  • SPF 50 broad-spectrum UVA/UVB protection
  • Shields against blue light and infrared radiation
  • Niacinamide visibly reduces dark spots and uneven tone
  • Hyaluronic acid for continuous hydration throughout the day
  • Ultra-light, invisible texture — no white cast

Aqua, Ethylhexyl Methoxycinnamate, Zinc Oxide, Glycerin, Niacinamide, Isononyl Isononanoate, Hyaluronic Acid, Titanium Dioxide, Dimethicone, Tocopheryl Acetate, Panthenol, Allantoin, Citric Acid, Phenoxyethanol, Ethylhexylglycerin, Parfum.

Apply liberally to face and neck every morning as the last step of your skincare routine. Gently blend outward from the centre of your face. Reapply every 2 hours during prolonged sun exposure.

  • Cleanse and tone skin first
  • Apply serum if using
  • Finish with this product as your moisturiser + SPF step
  • Reapply if swimming or exercising outdoors
